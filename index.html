Musical Stars is

an after school musical theatre class for children aged 5-16 years, run by professionals working in the industry.

The Musical Stars experience not only ignites a passion for Performing Arts but also gives children an opportunity to be challenged and to succeed. It encourages the formation of many important life skills and supports the NZ School Arts Curriculum.
